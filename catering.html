Let us cater your special events and celebrations

We cater to birthday parties, marriages, receptions, Pooja's and family gatherings. We always delivers the best service, expertise and selection for all of our events. We are happy to assist you in selecting the right type of menu for your event. We stand by the quality and quantity of food we provide. If our menus do not feature items that are towards your liking, do let us know, your choice of items will be added to your event and will be prepared to your preferences.

Enjoy the same mouthwatering delicious taste that you experienced at our restaurant, now at your place. Go ahead and throw that party that you have always wanted to now, without worrying about the food.
